About Kyani & Co.

Founded in 1904, Kyani & Co. is one of Mumbai’s oldest Irani cafes, offering an authentic Indo-Persian dining experience. Famous for its keema pav, Irani chai, and mouthwatering mawa cakes, the café serves generations of Mumbaiites who crave nostalgia and good food.
